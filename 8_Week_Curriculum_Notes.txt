GYM PERSONAL TRAINING
8-WEEK COMPREHENSIVE CURRICULUM
Online Classes • Fitness Training • Nutrition Guidance • Recovery Strategies
For All Fitness Levels

--- INTRODUCTION TO THE CURRICULUM ---
This 8-week comprehensive personal training curriculum is designed for fitness enthusiasts of all levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, this program will guide you through essential training principles, nutrition strategies, and recovery methods.

Curriculum Overview
- Strength training fundamentals and progressions
- Cardiovascular health and conditioning
- Nutrition science and meal planning
- Recovery optimization and injury prevention
- Body composition management
- Flexibility, mobility, and functional movement

--- WEEK 1: FOUNDATION & FITNESS ASSESSMENT ---
Lesson 1.1: Getting Started - Assessment & Goals
- Establish baseline fitness levels and set realistic goals.
- Body measurements: Weight, body fat %, chest, waist, hips, arms, thighs.
- Goal setting: SMART goals.

Lesson 1.2: Understanding Your Body
- Major muscle groups: Chest, back, shoulders, biceps, triceps, forearms.
- Core muscles & Lower Body mechanics.

Lesson 1.3: Nutrition Fundamentals
- Macronutrients: Proteins (muscle), Carbohydrates (energy), Fats (hormones).

--- WEEK 2: BUILDING STRENGTH FOUNDATION ---
Lesson 2.1: Proper Form & Technique
- Master fundamental movement patterns with proper form (Squat, Deadlift, Push-ups).

Lesson 2.2: Workout Programming Basics
- Progressive overload: Gradually increase weight or reps each week.

Lesson 2.3: Nutrition for Muscle Building
- Protein intake: 0.8-1g per pound of body weight daily.

--- WEEK 3: CARDIOVASCULAR CONDITIONING ---
Lesson 3.1: Cardio Fundamentals
- Heart rate zones, VO2 max, HIIT, and Steady-state.

Lesson 3.2: Balancing Cardio with Strength
- Frequency: 2-3 cardio sessions per week alongside strength training (20-30 mins).

--- WEEK 4: NUTRITION & DIET FUNDAMENTALS ---
Lesson 4.1: Calories & Energy Balance
- BMR & TDEE. Caloric deficits/surplus for fat loss or muscle gain.

Lesson 4.2: Meal Planning & Preparation
- Daily standard portions and supplementation guide (Whey, Creatine, Omega-3).

--- WEEK 5: ADVANCED STRENGTH TRAINING ---
Lesson 5.1: Progressive Overload Strategies
- Advanced techniques like Drop sets, Supersets, and Rest-pause sets.

--- WEEK 6: BODY COMPOSITION & FAT LOSS ---
Lesson 6.1: Fat Loss Principles
- Lose fat while preserving muscle using a moderate 300-500 calorie deficit.

--- WEEK 7: RECOVERY, MOBILITY & FLEXIBILITY ---
Lesson 7.2: Foam Rolling & SMR
- Reduce muscle tension and improve recovery on Rest Days.

--- WEEK 8: INTEGRATION & ADVANCED TECHNIQUES ---
Lesson 8.1: Putting It All Together
- Create a sustainable long-term fitness lifestyle.

*This document serves as your progressive guide across the entire 8 Weeks.*
